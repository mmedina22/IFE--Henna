What problems surround Henna?

Henna is an art that is not given its due respect. People do not appreciate the thorough hard-working artists, that study this everyday. Our goal is to make Henna a more appreicated and understood art to everyone, and allow more people to enjoy its elegance. We are giving people good quality and safe products. Many henna pastes are filled with chemicals that are harmful to skin and/or are very low quality products and do not stain your hands well. Our henna products will be made from henna leaf powder being the main ingredient along with water/lemon juice, sugar and essential oils (safe dye to make it multicolored). We will have workshops in community centers as well as retirement homes giving anyone the opportunity to learn this cool skills and improve. Everything for these workshops will be provided however there will be a small entry fee. We will also create an easy way for people to schedule appointments on our website for any occasion and even pay in advance.

What's this about?

Our key values are community, product quality, and commitment to the clients and each other. Our whole purpose is to provide people with safe and good quality products while opening them up to the world of henna and to give back to our community through our workshops.
